About Our Products

The True Face product is completely transparent with full-face visibility. Established in 2022, our clear, medical-grade mask has a smart and fashionable design optimized for comfort and breathability, while working at full capacity of protection. The True Face mask blocks atmospheric emission such as aerosol, fluids, and sprays from our faces through its transparent plastic barrier.

Unlike other masks, The True Face product allows us to see each other’s entire face and facial expressions, making communication more human, natural, and accessible. Transparent, see-through masks are important for all of us as we care about people who we love.

Our daily communication is mostly visual. Standard masks block faces and prevent our ability to see facial expressions and visual cues, lip-read, and fully communicate.

The ability to see a smile can absolutely help. Seeing the full face can help reduce confusion and improve relationships. The True Face transparent mask provides natural, empathetic communication and eliminates barriers caused by traditional masks.

Choose your own style with a wide variety of designs

Each mask is anti-fog and completely clear allowing you to show your smile, comfortable to wear utilizing elastic bands that are universal-fit ear-loops, and weigh lighter than a piece of letter sized paper (about 4.5 g) for absolute comfort when worn.
